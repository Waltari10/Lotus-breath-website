Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom breathing exercise?

Tap the exercise name at the bottom of the home screen, then tap "Create new exercise" to set your own inhale, hold, and exhale durations.

How does vibration guidance work?

When enabled, your phone vibrates in rhythm with each breathing phase — so you can close your eyes or put your phone in your pocket and still follow along. No headphones or screen needed.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Yes. Your exercises, history, and progress sync automatically via iCloud. Just sign in with the same Apple ID on both devices.

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one breathing exercise per day to maintain your streak. Streak freezes (available as an in-app purchase) protect your streak if you miss a day. You can hold up to 2 streak freezes at a time.

How do I save sessions to Apple Health?

Go to Settings > Sync to Apple Health and enable it. Completed breathing sessions will be saved as mindful minutes.

My data disappeared after reinstalling. What happened?

Your data syncs via iCloud. After reinstalling, the app waits for iCloud to sync your data back. Give it a minute, then reopen the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

What are XP Boosters?

XP Boosters temporarily multiply all XP earned from breathing exercises. They come in three tiers (1.5x, 2x, 3x) and help you level up faster. Available from the Donate page in the app.

Is the app free?

Yes. Lotus Breath is completely free with no ads. Optional in-app purchases (XP boosters and streak freezes) support continued development but are not required to use any feature.
